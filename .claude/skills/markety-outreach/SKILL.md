# Markety Outreach Skill

Write outreach content for Markety (marketyleadgen.com) — a pay-per-lead agency targeting local trade businesses.

## Kare's Voice & Style Rules
- **Short sentences.** Never rambling.
- **Hyphens** (-) not em/en dashes (—/–)
- **No "small"** — don't call businesses small
- **No fluff** — no "I hope this finds you well"
- **Casual but professional** — friendly, not salesy
- **Specific value upfront** — leads, website, free trial

## Core Offer to Communicate
1. We run Google + Facebook ads to get them more clients
2. We build them a **brand new website + landing page — included free** as long as they run leads with us
3. **Pay-per-lead only** — no fixed monthly cost, no risk
4. **First 30 days completely free**

## English Cold Email Template
**Subject**: Free website + leads for your [industry] business

**Body**:
```
Hi [Name],

I help [plural industry, e.g. "plumbers"] get more clients through Google and Facebook ads.

As part of the deal, we build you a brand new website and landing page - included for free as long as you're running leads with us.

You only pay per enquiry you receive (pay-per-lead), so no fixed monthly price and no risk.

The first 30 days are completely free.

Feel free to reply if this sounds interesting.
```

## Danish Cold Email Template
**Subject**: Gratis hjemmeside + leads til din [branche]

**Body**:
```
Hej [Navn],

Jeg hjælper [flertal, fx "VVS'ere"] med at få flere kunder via Google og Facebook-annoncer.

Som en del af aftalen bygger vi dig en helt ny hjemmeside og landingsside - inkluderet gratis, så længe du kører leads med os.

Du betaler kun pr. henvendelse du modtager (pay-per-lead), så ingen fast månedlig pris og ingen risiko.

De første 30 dage er helt gratis.

Skriv gerne tilbage, hvis det lyder interessant.
```

## Follow-Up (3-5 days later)
```
Hi [Name],

Just following up on my last message.

Have you had a chance to look at it?

Happy to answer any questions.
```

## LinkedIn Message (shorter)
```
Hi [Name], I help [industry] businesses get more clients through ads - and we build your website for free as part of it. Pay only per lead you get. First 30 days free. Worth a quick chat?
```

## Common Objections
- **"We already have a website"** → "That's great - ours is built specifically to convert ad traffic into leads. Your current site can stay, this one runs alongside it."
- **"We're too busy"** → "That's exactly when pay-per-lead makes sense - you control the lead volume. Turn it up or down."
- **"How much per lead?"** → "Depends on your industry and area - usually £20-80 per qualified enquiry. Book a call and I'll give you a specific number."
